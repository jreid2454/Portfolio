The Resume Blog in a Nutshell

The Resume Blog is a blog for my resume, and is for others to take inspiration from for their
resumes. Also, to be used as a platform to communicate with others seeking employment as a
software / web developer and share one another's experiences and tips. I hope you enjoy your
experience and I encourage anyone to make a post leaving feedback if they have advice or
constructive criticism they would like to share with not only me, but anyone looking into this field.
Thank you and enjoy!
